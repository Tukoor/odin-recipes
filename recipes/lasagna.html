Ingredients Needed

  • linguine
  • grean peas
  • carrots
  • mini corn chunks
  • a sauce of your choice. Feel free to use your imagination

Preparing the Dish

  1. We will be starting off by cooking the linguine in boiling water of your choice.
  2. As the linguine starts to cook, head out to your garden and pick some fresh peas and carrots. dress them as if they were to be the "belle of da ball".
  3. Once the vegetables are all dapper, throw them into the boiling water with the linguine.
  4. Use your best judgement to tell when everything is cooked, as i have no actual knowledge when it comes to cooking.
  5. Once everything is cooked, lay the linguine onto some fresh foil (use your preferred brand).
  6. mix the veggies into the sauce that you chose in the beginning and layer it all ontop of the linguine.
  7. Congragulations! Your dish is ready to serve, and you are now ready to induce your guests with projectile vomiting.
