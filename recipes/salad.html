Deceasar Salad

Description

This dish is a simple one, yet so magnificent. Low prep/cook time with high reward.

Ingredients Needed

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Croutons x4
  • to-go cup of ceasar dressing (or your prefferred dressing..but then it won't really be a ceasar salad)

Preparing the Dish

  1. Wash a few pieces of romaine lettuce. No less than 3 but no more than 5 is preferred.

  2. Defrost 4 singular pieces of that yummy crunch known as crouton.

  3. Lay out your favorite piece of to-go dinnerwear and place accordingly. *note. do not place croutons on top of lettuce or it will destroy the aesthetic and your dish wil not be "gram"-worthy.

  4. Voila! Dinner is served.
